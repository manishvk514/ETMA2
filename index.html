scn. 02 — position / take 01

We're a small studio.

We make brand films, music videos, trailers, and original work for the kind of client who's tired of watching the industry cut corners and call it progress.

We use whatever makes the image we see in our heads. Cameras, lights, edits, comps, CG, AI. None of it is the work. The work is what we decide to show you, and what we decide to leave out.

It's not a new argument. It's just the one that still matters.

scn. 04 — capabilities / take 01

What's on the table.

i.

Brand films

Launches, campaigns, anthems. Built the way scripted work is built — with a director, a point of view, and a cut that earns every second.

ii.

Music videos

A song deserves a world. We build the world, then let the track live inside it.

iii.

Trailers & titles

The first minute is the film. We cut for the minute that gets people into the room.

iv.

Motion & finishing

The full post pipeline, from comp to color. AI gets integrated where it serves the frame. Nowhere else.

v.

Visual development

Concept, look, pre-viz, the visual bible. The world before the shoot.

vi.

Anything else worth making

Short films, originals, formats that don't have a name yet. If it's built frame by frame, we're in.

if it needs to be directed, it belongs here.

scn. 05 — method / take 01

Before we make a frame,
we make a decision.

01 — intent

Decide what the viewer should feel.

Before references. Before tools. Before a single image. The emotion the work has to land on, written down in language a person can argue with. Everything after this serves that sentence.

02 — world

Build the universe on paper.

Palette. Lens. Grade. Light. Grain. The tactile quality of the image. We design the world the work lives in before we make anything. The world is the brief. Execution is what comes after.

03 — frame

Compose every shot.

Boarded shot by shot, with the deliberateness a DP brings to a set. Light falls where it should. Subjects exist where they must. The accidents are the ones we chose.

04 — motion

Control what moves.

A pause is as directed as a punch. Micro-expression, camera drift, the weight of the beat before the cut. Motion is how an image becomes a feeling.

05 — finish

Deliver when it's right.

Color, sound, and the final cut get the same scrutiny as everything upstream. We deliver when the work stops asking for changes. Not when the calendar asks for delivery.

scn. 06 — manifesto / take 01

A short list
of things we believe.

That the frame is still sacred, even when it's easier than ever to make one.

That a reference is not a brief, and a brief is not a film.

That the tools have no opinion about what they make. That's our job.

That fast and good are not the same thing, and pretending they are is how the work got boring.

That a machine cannot be surprised by its own work. A person can.

That taste is the thing no tool will ever learn.

That restraint is a craft. Knowing what to leave out is most of it.

That the film is in the cut, not in the shoot.

That if the room goes quiet when it plays, we did the job.

This is not for everyone.

And we think you already know whether it's for you.
